What's spree about?

Spree is an expert search engine where users ask questions to find other participating users, who are knowledgeable in that area and willing to help.

Spree facilitates real-time communication between users and experts via its chat interface.

Spree aims to create an active online community, especially in enterprises, where every user can ask questions or act as an expert helping fellow users.

Spree project is being jointly developed by Deutsche Telekom Laboratories and Dai-Labor, Technical University Berlin.

How can spree help me?

Spree helps you find answers you can't find on the web. When you ask a question in spree, it intelligently matches it to people who are knowledgeable about the subject.

It's a great way to network with fellow professionals and get answers and advice from knowledgeable sources.
