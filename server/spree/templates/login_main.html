spree creates an active online community where everybody shares knowledge and benefits from other users' knowledge alike. The spree technology automatically channels user questions to competent fellow users who are willing to help.

spree enables real-time communication between knowledge seekers and appropriate experts via its chat interface. In addition, users may edit notebooks or store chat histories and thus contribute to a constantly growing knowledge base.
